About us

Retro Mania is an American hat brand created in 2015 in San Francisco by two huge fans of the retrowave aesthetic and culture.

Anthony Bardinter - CEO and founder of Retro mania

Born in Philadelphia, Anthony is a former UX Designer who used to work for big tech companies like Amazon and Google in San Francisco for 6 years.

He discovered the retrowave culture in 2013 through the internet, and became quickly addicted to it.

Driven by the desire to become an entrepreneur, he decided to create his own company with his best friend Marta Hernandez.

Marta Hernandez - Business Manager and co-founder of Retro mania

Marta is a well-known Mexican tattoo artist established in San Francisco City since 2009.

As a long-term friend of Anthony, she has been introduced to the retrowave culture through him and she has done lots of hard work to grow their company with him through the years.

Marta also owns two tattoo parlors in New York City and in the South West of San Francisco, under the name of Marta Tats Shop.

Anthony and Marta are both really concerned about delivering quality products to all the retrowave lovers around the US.
