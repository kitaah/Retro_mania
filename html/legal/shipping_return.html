Shipping

How does your delivery process works ?

We only ship our products to the US.

All the packages are sent via DHL Express (Monday through Saturday). On average, packages are shipped 2 business days after an order is placed.

Once an order has been shipped, it usually takes 3-5 business days to fulfil it. You will also receive a shipping confirmation email containing an order number and a tracking number.

Once your package has been delivered, you will received a confirmation email.

I received a confirmation mail regarding the package delivery, but I still don’t have it. What should I do ?

Before contacting us, we strongly advise you to take all the following actions :

  • Check if the delivery address indicated in your shipping or delivery confirmation email is correct
  • Ask your local post office if they have your package
  • Check with your neighbours who may have received the package

If you have tried to do all the actions previously mentioned, without a solution, get in touch with us as quickly as possible at support@retromania.com with your order number or your tracking number.

We only accept claims filed within a period of 2 weeks from the delivery date.

Return

How does your return process works ?

If you want to return any items, please email us at support@retromania.com within a period of 5 weeks from the delivery date, with the following information :

  • Order number
  • Item(s) for return

To be eligible for a return, your item(s) must in the exact same condition that you received it.

For any eligible returns, you will have to pay the shipping fees and include in your package your invoice and the return form completed that we sent you in the first place with the item(s). You must return the item(s) in the original packaging.

After we receive your package, it will take 1 week to process your return. Once it is done, we will email you to notify your refund. It will take 15 days days to see refunds reflected on your credit card statement.

We only perform exchanges for wrong or damaged items.

I received a wrong or damaged item, what is your return policy for this type of situation ?

We offer refunds to all the customers that received damaged goods or the wrong goods. If this is the case for you, please send us and email at support@retromania.com with pictures of the wrong/damaged item and your order number or tracking number. The return shipping costs will be offered to you, we will email you a prepaid shipping label to print.

You can also email us if you want to exchange a damaged or wrong product, instead of getting a refund. We only accept to exchange it for the same product.

We only accept claims filed within a period of 2 weeks from the delivery date.
